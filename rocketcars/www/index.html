HOW TO PLAY

DRIVING

Left thumb anywhere on the left half is a floating stick — left/right steers.

GAS / BRAKE drive forward and reverse. DRIFT powerslides around corners.

BOOST burns your boost meter for speed — and it is the only way to fly.

Drive over yellow pads for +12 boost, big orange rings for a full 100.

AIR & TRICKS

JUMP to hop — hold it for a higher jump.

DOUBLE JUMP — tap JUMP again in the air with the stick centred.

FLIP / DODGE — tap JUMP again while holding a direction. Forward flips hit hardest.

FRONT FLICK — balance the ball on your nose, then flip forward into it: the spin launches it.

FLY — jump, aim the nose with the stick, then hold BOOST. Hold DRIFT in the air to air-roll.

You can drive up the walls and across the ceiling if you carry enough speed.
